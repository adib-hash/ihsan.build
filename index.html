Hello.

I'm Adib — a generalist who operates at the intersection of business, technology, and ideas. I currently serve as President of Alliance Solutions Group, a consulting firm building software for the construction and real estate industries.

Before this, I spent time at Evergreen as an investor and operating executive, and have been involved in governance at the Center for Open Science and the Miller Center at UVA.

I'm drawn to hard problems, good books, and questions that don't have clean answers. This site is a small window into what I'm thinking about, building, and reading.
